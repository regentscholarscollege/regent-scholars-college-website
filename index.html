Admissions

A formal admissions process built around route selection, document review, and guided next steps.

1. Submit an enquiry

Contact the admissions team to discuss your current qualifications and intended progression route.

2. Confirm entry route

Select the most appropriate entry point: Foundation, Level 3, or Business Management Level 4 and 5.

3. Prepare documents

Provide transcripts, prior qualification records, and any additional materials requested during review.

4. Receive guidance

Work with the college on admissions review, offer guidance, and the next stage of enrolment planning.
